Boots and gets past the splash screen, trying to play the intro. If you wait for about 45 minutes, it will attempt to load the brightness selection screen.
Versions 1.000 and 1.004 are able to go this far, but 1.003 crashes after the splash screen and 1.005 fails to boot.
The log included is up to when it goes past the splash screen.
